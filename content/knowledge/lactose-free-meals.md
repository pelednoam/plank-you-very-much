### Simple LF Options

*   **Breakfast:** Oatmeal with berries and almond milk.
*   **Lunch:** Grilled chicken salad with vinaigrette.
*   **Dinner:** Salmon with roasted vegetables.
*   **Snack:** Apple slices with peanut butter.
