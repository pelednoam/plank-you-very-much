### 5-Minute Warm-up

1.  **Cardio:** 2 min jumping jacks or similar.
2.  **Dynamic Stretching:** Arm circles, leg swings.
3.  **Finger Tendons:** Gentle pulls with rubber band.
4.  **Easy Routes:** Start with 1-2 very easy climbs.
